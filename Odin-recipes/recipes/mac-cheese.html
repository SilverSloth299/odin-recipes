Mac & Cheese

Description

Classic Mac & Cheese recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g macaroni pasta
  • 1½ cups milk
  • 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water; drain.
  2. In the same pot, melt butter over low heat.
  3. Add milk and stir in cheese until melted.
